What to Expect

Weekend Services

We are blessed with a growing and vibrant church family, and as a result, we have some wonderful problems! 

On Sundays, we offer three services back-to-back with the same speaker. There are often lines to enter the service while the previous service is being released.

The 8:00am and 1:00pm services are the ones we recommend if you want a higher chance of finding a seat! Another option is the 9:15am service at our Twin View campus, which provides a smaller venue.

Please don’t attend more than one service in a row on Sunday mornings, as there are more people coming in right behind you who have traveled far and wide to get here, just like you!

We have visitors from all over the world each weekend, so get to know the amazing people you are sitting by!

Parking

As you arrive, follow the direction of our friendly parking team to get you to an open spot.

When attending services or events at the primary Bethel campus, please don’t park in the lower gravel lot. Due to limited space on campus, we don’t recommend motorhomes and RVs, but if you can’t avoid driving them to service, this is the only exception to parking in the lower gravel lot.

No pets are allowed on campus, so please make other accommodations for your furry friends.

Be prepared for a bit of traffic on the way out, as well! You’ll find this to be Redding’s only traffic jam… so it’s kind of a novelty.

Children

We believe there is no junior Holy Spirit, so we expect your kids will absolutely experience God’s presence in their classrooms!

Parents are asked to check in and pick up children up to 6th grade. Visitor check in can be found through the south lobby entrance at the end of the hallway, and starts 30 minutes before services. Please allow an extra 15-20 minutes to check in your child.

For more information visit our Children's Ministry page.
